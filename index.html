Sesame Seed Balls




Craving a tasty but healthy snack? Well, this ball of joy is the perfect treat for you! Its made with simple and tasty ingredients, like dates and sesame seeds. There is no added sugar in this recipe, so it won't make you feel guilty when you snack on these! This recipe is also costomizable to your preferances; if you don't like dates, try replacing it with figs! It gives a similar taste and feel, and won't make much of a difference. Have fun making it, and enjoy this delicious treat! ~ Aishani U.




Ingredients:

    pitted dates,1 lbs

    butter (at room temperature) 1/4 cup

    1/4 cup water

    1/2 lbs blanched almonds

    1 tsp vanilla extract

    1 tsp ground cardamom

    1/2 tsp ground cinnamon

    1/2 cup toasted sesame seeds




Instructions:

    1) Place the dates, butter, water, almonds, vanilla, cardamom,sesame seeds, and cinnamon in a food processor. Pulse to form a smooth paste. (not liguidy, just a little doughy)

    2) Shape the dough into date-size balls.

    3) Enjoy!
